Banana Pudding

Description

Everyone needs a good banana pudding recipe in their repertoire â€” and this one makes by far the easiest, most delicious banana pudding you'll ever come across.

Ingredients

  • Milk and pudding mix
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Vanilla
  • Frozen whipped topping
  • Vanilla wafers
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Whisk the pudding mix into the milk.
  2. Add the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla.
  3. Fold in the frozen whipped topping.
  4. Layer the mixture with the sliced bananas and vanilla wafers.
